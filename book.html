The Pinochet Shock
Radical Economic Change and Life Under Dictatorship in Chile
Edited by Felipe González and Mounu Prem
Palgrave Macmillan, 2025

This edited volume brings together multidisciplinary work on the economic, political, and social consequences of the Pinochet dictatorship in Chile.
